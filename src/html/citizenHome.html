About PICMS

Serving Karachi with
Integrity & Honour

PICMS is a digital initiative by Karachi Police to modernise how citizens interact with law enforcement — bringing transparency, speed and accountability to every investigation across all 6 districts of Karachi.

  • Serving over 20 million citizens under the Sindh Police Act
  • Operating 47 stations across all 6 districts of Karachi
  • Reducing case processing time by up to 60% through digitalisation
  • 24/7 encrypted citizen access with strict confidentiality protocols
  • Part of the Sindh Government's Smart Policing Initiative 2025
